Building on Nearly 40 Years of Lending Experience

CRL's work grows directly from our affiliation with Self-Help, our founder and one of the nation's largest nonprofit community development lenders. For nearly 40 years, Self-Help has worked to create ownership and economic opportunity in underserved communities through responsible loans and financial services.
