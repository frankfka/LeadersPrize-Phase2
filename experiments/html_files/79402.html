Towpath Trail 2020

Help complete the Towpath Trail by 2020

 

Support the Towpath Trail

Do you hike or bicycle with your family, exercise or walk your dog on the Ohio & Erie Canal Towpath Trail? If you are one of the 2,000,000 users of this beautiful hiking trail, please help us complete the Towpath Trail by making a contribution to our annual fundraising campaign.

 

Our Goal

Our goal is to complete the Towpath Trail from Cleveland to New Philadelphia, Ohio by the year 2020, and we need your help! To date, 80 of the 101 miles of the Towpath Trail are completed, and the remaining 21 miles will be our challenging and costly to build. The average cost per mile of trail is $750,000.

Our fundraiser goal for 2013 is $30,000 and we need your help to reach our fundraising goal. Through your generous contribution, we will build the Towpath Trail and develop a legacy for future generations!
