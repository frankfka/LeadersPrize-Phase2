Professor Craig Maher, Office Director

 

Associate Professor
Ph.D. in Political Science, University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee (1995), Public Policy and Finance.

Dr. Maher regularly teaches the following courses:
State and Local Politics (MPA 726)
Quantitative and Qualitative Research Methods (MPA 732)
Public Budgeting and Finance (MPA 752)
Revisiting Bureaucracy (MPA 797)

Dr. Maher was recently appointed to Wisconsin Governor Doyle's Talsk Force on Milwaukee County Finances, and to the National League of Cities and Urban Institutes' Legislating for Results Advisory Board, in addition to being an elected official on the Wauwatosa Common Council. He is widely published in the field of public finance.

To contact Dr. Maher, try any of the following options:

  • Visit him at his office: Clow Faculty 408
  • Call him at his office: 920-424-7304
  • Email him: maher@uwosh.edu
