Tune into DCTV Channel 23 on Comcast Cable in DeKalb County, or check us out here on our website. We are dedicated to bringing you news, services and events uniquely focused on DeKalb County.

DCTV shares information in a way in which everyone can understand how DeKalb County government serves its stakeholders. Our programming focuses specifically on showcasing the events, programs, services, and activities that positively impact the lives of DeKalb County residents, employees and visitors.
