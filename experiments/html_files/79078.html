Chris Abele Statement on Milwaukee County Executive Forum

MILWAUKEE — Milwaukee County Executive Candidate Chris Abele released the following statement after the conclusion of the Milwaukee Press Club County Executive candidate forum at Marquette University School of Law.

“Today I was grateful to share my plans about how I will work with anyone who has good ideas to create private sector jobs whether they are Democrats or Republicans,” said Abele. “I look forward to talking to voters over the course of the campaign about how we need new leadership and a change in culture if we are going to create jobs and protect our services by doing more, better for less.”

Chris Abele is a civic leader, businessman and community advocate who has worked to strengthen Milwaukee County by helping to build and grow programs in education, the environment, health services and the arts. He helped lead an audit of the county’s pension problems, and identified an idea that will save taxpayers millions of dollars.

Chris proudly lives in Milwaukee with his wife Miriam and their daughter, Lauren.

For more about Chris Abele, visit www.chrisabele.com
