Read the Full Text of the Fourth Democratic Debate in Charleston

By Ryan Teague Beckwith
Updated: January 18, 2016 2:18 AM ET

Three candidates met for the fourth Democratic debate in Charleston, South Carolina, Sunday night.

Former Secretary of State Hillary Clinton, Vermont Sen. Bernie Sanders and former Maryland Gov. Martin O’Malley appeared at the debate, which was hosted by NBC News and YouTube and moderated by anchor Lester Holt.
