BUDGET SURPLUS SHOWS REFORMS MAKING GIANT STRIDES TOWARDS PROSPERITY

Submitted by nathan.conrad on Fri, 05/11/2012 - 10:23am
Tweet
 

Before Governor Walker took office in January of 2011, the state faced an astronomical structural deficit. At $3.6 billion, the people of the state of Wisconsin we’re on the hook for one of the largest per capita deficits in the country.

Governor Walker went immediately to work to tackle this Mt. Everest sized problem and put a stop to the fleecing of the taxpayer’s pocketbooks.

In just over a year we have seen this bloated, pork-laden deficit demolished and completely eliminated. Not only was this giant burden to the taxpayers effectively erased, but it was done without raising taxes on the hardworking people of Wisconsin.

Yesterday, news broke that the state budget is actually ahead of the game when it comes to lowering the burden on the people of Wisconsin. The state Department of Administration released a report, sadly to little fanfare, that shows the state is actually on pace to have a $154.4 million surplus.

This is a tremendous shot in the arm for Wisconsinites. Not only have we turned away from the failed policies that drove us to the brink of insolvency with chronic billion-dollar budget deficits, double-digit tax increases and record-setting job loss, but we have taken a complete 180 degree turn to true prosperity with a budget surplus that will help far into our future.

For the first time in state history, we have put millions of dollars into our rainy day fund in consecutive years. To be specific, this is only the third time in ten years that we have actually added money to the fund that will help us if an unforeseen catastrophic event were to occur. At $45.4 this investment in our rainy day fund is one of the largest influxes of state funds to the stabilization fund in Wisconsin history.

More telling is the fact that the $60.18 million in rainy day funds invested since Governor Walker has taken office has surpassed the total investments made by the previous administration over former Governor Doyle’s disastrous eight year tenure as our state’s chief executive.

The report, which you can read in its entirety here, is further proof that the bold and forward thinking reforms that Governor Walker has put in place has laid a solid financial foundation for the future of our state and that we are on the road to prosperity that will continue to move Wisconsin Forward.

Nathan Conrad
Deputy Communications Director
